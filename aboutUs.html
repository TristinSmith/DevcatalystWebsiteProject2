In 2007, our founder, Jacob Stevens, grew tired of the price for 3D printed goods; due to this, Jacob vowed to make a company to create and ship affodable printed goods. Around 2009, Jacob and a group of friends decided to start D^3 in one of their garages. This allowed costs to remain low and kept orders coming in.
