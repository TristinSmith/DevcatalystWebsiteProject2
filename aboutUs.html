December, 2008: Our founder, Jacob Stevens, grew tired of the price for 3D printed goods; due to this, Jacob vowed to make a company to create and ship affodable printed goods. He bought a 3D printer and began to operate locally.


March, 2009: Jacob and a group of friends decided to start D³ in one of their garages. They increased production greatly, allowing the company to operate across the entire county and bordering regions of neighboring counties.


July, 2010: The group bought a warehouse in order to facilitate their 3D printing needs. Despite facing many harships and the Legal System, D³ began to expand rapidly.


October, 2010: The company expanded to multiple locations in multiple states, cementing themselves as a heavy competitor in the 3D Printing Market.
